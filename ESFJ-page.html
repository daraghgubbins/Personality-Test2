Extraverted Sensing Feeling Judging

Summary

• Social • Warmhearted • People person • Determined • Loyal • Popular • Responsible

The ESFJ is a very common personality type. ESFJ's are popular and do their best to get on with everyone. They do their best to do the right thing, an ESFJ is very responsible and respects authority. Nothing makes an ESFJ happier than when they get to help another person.
